## About the App
Voice Notes AI allows users to record voice notes and generate AI-powered summaries and actionable task lists.

## AI & Data Processing
Voice recordings and transcripts are processed to generate summaries using AI services.
No user accounts are required.
Data is not sold or used for advertising.
User data is processed only to provide app functionality and is not used to train AI models.

## Troubleshooting
If the app fails to generate a summary, please ensure you have an active internet connection.

---

## Privacy Policy

Voice Notes AI respects your privacy.

### Data Collection
- Voice recordings and transcripts are processed solely to provide app functionality such as transcription, summaries, and task extraction.
- The app does not collect personal information for advertising or tracking purposes.

### Data Usage
- Audio and text data are used only to generate summaries and actionable tasks.
- Data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing or analytics.

### Data Storage
- Notes are stored locally on the user’s device.
- Temporary processing may occur on secure servers to generate AI-powered features.

### Tracking
- Voice Notes AI does not track users across apps or websites.
